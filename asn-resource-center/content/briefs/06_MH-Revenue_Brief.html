ASN Data Brief—Nephrology Key Contributor to Hospital Revenue

Newly released survey data collected from 62 hospital CFOs found nephrology makes key contributions to hospital systems gross revenue. The report from Merrit Hawkins also found the ratio of return on investment (ROI) for nephrologists’ median salary was higher than gastroenterology, cardiology, and pulmonology.
