The Chronic Kidney Disease (CKD) Surveillance System is overseen by the CDC to track kidney diseases in the US. The Surveillance System offers numerous resources, including:

  • CKD Risk Calculators Interactive tool to estimate individual risk for CKD based on multiple factors.

  • Prevalence and Incidence Data Aggregate data on prevalence and incidence by CKD Stage.
